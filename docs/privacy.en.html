HanTalk ("the Service") complies with applicable data protection laws including the GDPR and Korea's Personal Information Protection Act. We collect the minimum data necessary and delete it as soon as its purpose is fulfilled.

2. Handling of Chat Content (No-Storage Principle)

  • Messages (text/drawings) pass through the server only to be delivered, and are deleted immediately upon delivery confirmation.
  • Undelivered messages are automatically deleted from the server within 24 hours.
  • Delivered messages are stored only on each user's device and auto-deleted after 24 hours.
  • The operator therefore cannot read or recover past chat content.

3. Contacts

  • Contacts permission is requested only when you use friend-finding.
  • Phone numbers are converted to a one-way hash (SHA-256) on-device before matching; the raw number and names are never sent to the server.
  • Your friend list is stored on your device only, not on the server.

4-2. International Transfer

Our infrastructure (Firebase) operates in the United States (Google LLC). Accordingly, the data in Section 1 is transferred to and processed in the US, protected by EU Standard Contractual Clauses (SCC) and Google's data-protection certifications.

4-3. Children's Data

The Service does not accept users under 14 (Korea), under 16 in the EEA (or the applicable member-state age), or under 13 (US COPPA). Any children's data discovered is deleted without delay.

5. Deletion

Data whose retention period has passed or whose purpose is fulfilled is deleted without delay and irreversibly. Data required to be kept by law is stored separately and deleted upon expiry.

You may at any time request access, correction, deletion, restriction, or portability of your data via the in-app account-closure feature or the contact below. Requests are handled without delay (within 1 month at the latest).
